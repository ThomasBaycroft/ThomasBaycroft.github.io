Publications

Hi, I'm Tom, I am a PhD student in Astronomy and Astrophysics at the University of Birmingham, UK.
My research interests lie mainly in the field of Exoplanet science, discovering planets around stars other than our Sun, in particular planets orbiting around pairs of stars.
